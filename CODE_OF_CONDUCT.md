# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

PulsorClip is maintained as a pragmatic, respectful, and technically serious project.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate clearly and respectfully
- focus feedback on code, product behavior, and documented decisions
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, or hostile behavior
- respect legal and platform constraints when discussing downloader workflows

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following is not acceptable:

- harassment or intimidation
- abusive or insulting language
- spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption
- sharing illegal usage instructions for third-party content

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors who violate these standards.
